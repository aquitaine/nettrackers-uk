## The Challenge

A four-partner law firm specialising in employment and commercial law had a website built in 2016 — no HTTPS, no mobile optimisation, and a WordPress theme that loaded in 8+ seconds. They were invisible on Google for every relevant keyword.

The goal: build a fast, authoritative web presence that generates qualified enquiries without ongoing PPC spend.

## Our Approach

### Site Architecture

We started with keyword research across their three practice areas: employment law, commercial contracts, and dispute resolution. From this we designed a flat URL architecture — no more than two levels deep — with every practice area page targeting a clear search intent.

### Technical Build

We built the new site in Next.js with App Router, deployed to Vercel's edge network. Key technical decisions:

- **Static generation** for all service and content pages — TTFB under 80ms
- **Schema markup**: LegalService, LocalBusiness, FAQPage, and BreadcrumbList across all pages
- **Image pipeline**: all attorney photos and office images through `next/image` with AVIF/WebP output
- **No WordPress** — MDX for all blog content, zero plugin vulnerabilities

### Content & Authority

We wrote 22 new service pages and 14 blog posts targeting long-tail legal queries (e.g. "unfair dismissal claim London", "commercial lease review solicitor"). Each page included:
- Clear E-E-A-T signals with solicitor bylines
- FAQPage schema for featured snippet capture
- Internal linking to related service pages

### Local SEO

We set up and optimised their Google Business Profile, built citations across 30 legal directories, and implemented LocalBusiness schema pointing to their City of London office.
