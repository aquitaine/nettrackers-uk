## The Challenge

A London-based B2B SaaS company (project management tooling for professional services) had tried two previous SEO agencies with disappointing results. They were spending on content that wasn't ranking, and had no clear view of which keywords were driving trials or MQLs.

Starting point:
- 12 MQLs/month from organic
- No rank tracking in place
- Blog content with no keyword strategy
- Site on an outdated CMS with slow page speeds

## What We Did Over 12 Months

### Month 1–2: Foundation

Full technical audit and rebuild of their tracking. We implemented:
- Google Search Console property set with correct domain verification
- GA4 with custom events for trial signups, demo requests, and doc downloads
- Custom rank tracking dashboard (built in Node.js + Google Sheets API) for 200 target keywords
- Resolved 14 technical issues including hreflang errors, paginated duplicate content, and missing canonical tags

### Month 3–5: Architecture and Quick Wins

We redesigned their site's information architecture to match the keyword clusters we'd identified — separating feature pages from use-case pages and adding comparison pages targeting "[competitor] alternative" searches.

Quick wins: 28 existing pages optimised and re-indexed within 6 weeks, leading to a 40% increase in impressions.

### Month 6–9: Content Engine

We built a repeatable content process: keyword brief → outline review → draft → SEO edit → publish. Publishing 6 articles/month, every piece targeting a specific stage of the buyer journey.

Notable content wins:
- "Project management for consultants" — position 2, driving 800 visits/month
- "[Competitor] vs [Client]" comparison pages — 3 pages on page 1 within 60 days

### Month 10–12: Custom Tooling

We built a lightweight internal SEO dashboard that pulled GSC, GA4, and Ahrefs data into a single Slack-delivered weekly report. The client's marketing team could see ranking movements, traffic trends, and MQL attribution without opening five tools.

## Results

| Metric | Month 1 | Month 12 |
|--------|---------|---------|
| Organic MQLs/month | 12 | 51 |
| Target keywords top 5 | 11% | 68% |
| Monthly organic sessions | 3,200 | 18,900 |
| Trial signups from organic | 38 | 171 |
| Organic as % of total pipeline | 8% | 34% |

Organic became the company's top-performing acquisition channel by Month 10, surpassing paid search for the first time.
